About Alar veliyeedu

It is indeed true that the human species is in no way superior to other living beings. However, the ability to deeply appreciate life, analyze it with intellect, and discern between good and bad makes humans a unique species. Recognizing that reading is crucial for such a dignified life, the publishing house Alar Publications was established.

Our goal is to encourage reading by publishing quality books at affordable prices for the majority of people. Alar Publications takes pride in emphasizing that its uniqueness lies in not focusing solely on commercial objectives but in acting with a sense of responsibility.
