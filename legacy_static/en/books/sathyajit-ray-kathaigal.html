Sathyajit Ray

Sathyajit Ray (1921–1992) was a master Indian filmmaker, screenwriter, documentary filmmaker, author, composer, lyricist, magazine editor, and illustrator.

His contribution to the film industry is world-renowned. In the literary world, he holds an immortal place through children's fiction, detective stories, and science fiction. Characters like Feluda and Professor Shonku were created by him.

Although his literary works are in a simple style, they contain profound ideas and interesting twists.
