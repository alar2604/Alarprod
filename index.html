About Alar veliyeedu

Alar veliyeedu is one of the fastest growing Tamil language book publisher in the Tamilnadu, publishing over 250 new titles every year and with an active backlist of over 3000 titles.

With a prestigious list including Booker Prize winners, Nobel laureates, and beloved authors, we are dedicated to publishing acclaimed fiction and non-fiction for adults and children.

Our mission: To ignite a universal passion for reading by creating books for everyone.
